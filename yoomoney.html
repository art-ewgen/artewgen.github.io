YooMoney (ex.Yandex.Money) Design processes of payment solutions in YooKassa and B2C utility bills project

YooMoney (ex Yandex.Money) company is a Russian electronic payment service that helps users pay for goods and services, and companies accept online payments. YooMoney is the most popular service in Runet in accepting payments on the Internet, based on the number of sites, MARC data for 2019.

Scope of work

During my work, I redesigned projects such as the Embedded checkout form (Widget), Redirect form and Mobile SDK. Also, I implemented a new method in payment forms — SberPay.

Responsibilities:

  • conducting user researches: interviews and usability testings;
  • taking an active role in the sprints planning for the front-end team for effective production of services and using scrum methodology;
  • developing a design system;
  • mentoring new product designers.

Checkout Widget Redesign

YooMoney widget is the company's latest payment acceptance solution. It's a ready-made form for websites that provides seamless payments without switching to other tabs. The widget is used by Adidas & Reebok, iHerb, Cian, Renault and other big names.

Problems and solutions

The previous version of the widget is outdated, did not suit users and businesses, it was decided to make a redesign. After conducting the user interviews and tests, collecting their wishes and problems, we identified major goals for the redesign: optimization, improvement and extension.

Results of redesign was the closure of all goals:

  • major product and UX issues are closed;
  • conversion rate increased more than 5%;
  • new features: loyalty, scalability, re-election etc.

Additional goal that the concept of the widget was re-used in more than 8 company projects.

You can find a full case of the widget redesign on Behance .
